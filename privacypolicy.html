# Privacy Policy for Nimbus: Weather Wallpaper **Last Updated: December 17, 2024** Nimbus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Nimbus: Weather Wallpaper (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. --- ## 1. Information We Collect ### 1.1 Location Data - **What we collect:** Your device's geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) - **Why we collect it:** To display accurate weather information for your current location and generate personalized weather-based artwork - **How it's used:** Location data is sent to third-party weather APIs to retrieve current weather conditions - **Storage:** Location data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers ### 1.2 Device Information - **What we collect:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers - **Why we collect it:** To optimize app performance, provide customer support, and process in-app purchases - **Storage:** Minimal device information may be stored locally on your device ### 1.3 Usage Data - **What we collect:** App interaction data, feature usage, generation counts, and preferences - **Why we collect it:** To improve the App experience and provide personalized features - **Storage:** Stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms ### 1.4 Purchase Information - **What we collect:** Transaction records for in-app purchases (subscriptions and credit packages) - **Why we collect it:** To manage your subscription status and credit balance - **How it's processed:** All payments are processed through Apple's App Store; we do not have access to your payment card details ### 1.5 Generated Images - **What we collect:** AI-generated weather artwork created within the App - **Storage:** Images are stored locally on your device and in your device's photo library (with your permission) - **Note:** We do not upload or store your generated images on our servers --- ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - Provide accurate, location-based weather information - Generate personalized AI weather artwork - Process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions - Track your credit balance and generation history - Save your preferences and settings - Improve and optimize the App's performance - Provide customer support - Comply with legal obligations --- ## 3. Third-Party Services Our App uses the following third-party services: ### 3.1 Google Gemini API - **Purpose:** AI image generation - **Data shared:** Text prompts describing weather conditions (no personal data) - **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy ### 3.2 Open-Meteo Weather API - **Purpose:** Weather data retrieval - **Data shared:** Geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) - **Privacy Policy:** https://open-meteo.com/en/terms ### 3.3 RevenueCat - **Purpose:** In-app purchase and subscription management - **Data shared:** Anonymous user identifiers, purchase history - **Privacy Policy:** https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy ### 3.4 Apple App Store - **Purpose:** Payment processing, app distribution - **Data shared:** As required by Apple for purchase processing - **Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ --- ## 4. Data Storage and Security ### 4.1 Local Storage - All user preferences, settings, and generated images are stored locally on your device - We use secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system - Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it ### 4.2 No Cloud Storage - We do not operate cloud servers that store your personal data - Generated images are not uploaded to any external servers - Your collection of saved images exists only on your device ### 4.3 Security Measures - API communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS - Sensitive data is stored using secure, encrypted local storage - We follow industry-standard security practices --- ## 5. Data Retention - **Local Data:** Stored until you delete the App or clear app data - **Purchase Records:** Maintained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their retention policies - **Weather Requests:** Processed in real-time; not retained after the response is delivered --- ## 6.
